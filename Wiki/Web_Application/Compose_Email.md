# Composing and Sending Emails

## How to Compose an Email

1. Click the **Compose** button
2. Enter recipient in **To** field (add **CC/BCC** if needed)
3. Add a **Subject** line
4. Type your message in the **Body**
5. Click the **Attach** icon to add files (optional)
6. Click **Send** to deliver the email

## Email Fields

- **To**: Required recipient email address
- **CC**: Carbon copy - visible to all recipients
- **BCC**: Blind carbon copy - hidden from other recipients
- **Subject**: Brief description of your email
- **Body**: Your main message content
- **Attachments**: Files you want to include

## Composing Your Email

### Adding Recipients

#### To Field (Required)
- **Type email addresses** directly
- **Select from contacts** if you have them saved
- **Multiple recipients** separated by commas
- **Auto-complete** suggestions as you type

#### CC (Carbon Copy)
- **Visible to all recipients** - everyone can see who else received the email
- **Use for transparency** when multiple people need to be informed
- **Optional field** - leave blank if not needed

#### BCC (Blind Carbon Copy)
- **Hidden from other recipients** - privacy protection for email addresses
- **Use for group emails** where recipients shouldn't see each other's addresses
- **Protecting privacy** in mass communications

### Writing Your Subject Line

#### Best Practices
- **Be specific and clear** about the email's purpose
- **Keep it concise** - aim for 50 characters or less
- **Use action words** when appropriate (Request, Update, Invitation)
- **Avoid all caps** which can appear as shouting

#### Examples of Good Subjects
- "Meeting request for project review - Tuesday 2pm"
- "Updated report attached for your review"
- "Question about account settings"
- "Follow-up on our conversation yesterday"

### Crafting Your Message

#### Message Structure
- **Greeting** - "Hello [Name]" or "Hi there"
- **Main content** - Your primary message
- **Call to action** - What you want the recipient to do
- **Closing** - "Best regards," "Thanks," etc.
- **Signature** - Your name and contact information

#### Writing Tips
- **Use clear, simple language** that's easy to understand
- **Break up long paragraphs** for better readability
- **Be concise** - respect your recipient's time
- **Include relevant details** but avoid unnecessary information
- **Proofread** before sending to catch typos and errors

## Adding Attachments

### Supported File Types

#### Documents
- **PDF files** for finalized documents
- **Word documents** (.doc, .docx)
- **Excel spreadsheets** (.xls, .xlsx)
- **PowerPoint presentations** (.ppt, .pptx)
- **Text files** (.txt, .rtf)

#### Images and Media
- **Photos** (.jpg, .png, .gif, .bmp)

### Attachment Guidelines

#### File Size Limits
- **Maximum file size**: 25MB per attachment
- **Total email size**: 25MB including all attachments
- **Large file alternatives**: Use cloud sharing links for bigger files

#### Best Practices
- **Compress large files** when possible
- **Use descriptive filenames** that recipients will understand
- **Virus scan** attachments before sending
- **Consider recipients' ability** to open file types

### Adding Attachments

#### Upload Process
1. **Click the attachment icon** (paperclip) in the compose window
2. **Browse your device** to select files
3. **Select multiple files** if needed (hold Ctrl/Cmd)
4. **Wait for upload** to complete before sending
5. **Remove attachments** by clicking the 'X' if needed

#### Attachment Preview
- **File names** and sizes displayed
- **Upload progress** shown during attachment process
- **Thumbnail previews** for images
- **File type icons** for easy identification

## Sending Your Email

### Before You Send

#### Final Review Checklist
- **Recipients are correct** - check To, CC, and BCC fields
- **Subject line is clear** and appropriate
- **Message is complete** with all necessary information
- **Attachments are included** if mentioned in the email
- **Spelling and grammar** have been checked

#### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- **Reply All misuse** - only use when everyone needs to see your response
- **Wrong recipients** - double-check email addresses
- **Missing attachments** - mention of attachments without including them
- **Unclear subject lines** that don't describe the content

### Send Options

#### Send Now
- **Click the Send button** to deliver immediately
- **Confirmation message** appears when successfully sent
- **Copy saved** to your Sent folder automatically

#### Send and Archive
- **Send and remove** from inbox in one action
- **Clean inbox management** for processed emails
- **Automatic organization** of sent items

## Reply and Forward

### Replying to Emails

#### Reply vs Reply All
- **Reply** - responds only to the sender
- **Reply All** - responds to sender and all original recipients
- **Choose wisely** to avoid unnecessary emails for others

#### Reply Features
- **Original message** included below your response
- **Automatic subject** with "Re:" prefix
- **Recipient fields** pre-filled from original email
- **Quote formatting** to distinguish original content

### Forwarding Emails

#### When to Forward
- **Sharing information** with someone who wasn't on the original email
- **Passing along requests** to the appropriate person
- **Distributing announcements** to your team or contacts

#### Forward Best Practices
- **Add context** explaining why you're forwarding
- **Remove irrelevant parts** of long email chains
- **Check permissions** before forwarding confidential information
- **Update subject line** if the focus changes
